Baked Rice

Baked Rice - one the best rice's recipes

Baked Rice is an easy and inexpensive recipe that you can make to yourself or to your guests. It has a lot of ingredientes but they're easy to mix up and they all come together to create an amazing taste in your mouth. Try it today and tell me your thoughts. You can contact me on my Email

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Smoked sausages
  • Canned corn
  • Canned Pea
  • Muzzarella cheese
  • Oregano

Steps

  1. Cook the rice
  2. Fry the sausages
  3. Mix up the rice, the sausages, canned corn and canned pea on a dish
  4. Top the dish with the muzzarella cheese and put oregano on top of the cheese
  5. Put the dish in the oven and wait until the muzzarella mels
  6. Take it out of the oven
  7. Enjoy!

Spaghetti with oil and garlic

My version of this easy and delicious recipe. Ideal dish for these days when you want a good meal but you don't want to have a lot of work.

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 2 spoons olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, sliced

Steps

  1. Cook the spaguetti in water for about 10min and drain it afterwards
  2. Put the olive oil on a pan and when you think it's very hot, add garlic
  3. Let the garlic fry
  4. Add the spaguetti on the pan with oil and garlic and mix it all up
  5. It's ready to serve!
